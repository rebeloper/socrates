# Socrates

## Experience Driven Development

**By Alex Nagy**

---

I started writing software long before AI could generate code.

Over the years, I've watched every major shift in our industry change how developers work. But this one feels different. For the first time, our biggest risk isn't falling behind technologically; it's slowly giving away the very thinking that made us valuable in the first place.

I wrote Socrates because I believe the future belongs to developers who can think better, not just type faster.

My mission is simple: help developers invest in the one thing AI can't replace: themselves.
